# Privacy Policy for CJR Performance App

**Last Updated: May 15, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

CJR Performance ("the App", "we", "us", "our") operates as a mobile application for vehicle performance tracking. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number, and user profile data
- **Vehicle Data**: Vehicle specifications, performance metrics, and usage statistics
- **Camera Data**: Photos and videos captured through the app's camera feature
- **Location Data**: GPS location when enabled for tracking purposes

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and app version
- **Usage Data**: Features accessed, interaction patterns, and crash logs
- **Analytics Data**: App performance metrics and user behavior analytics

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:
- Providing and improving app functionality
- Delivering personalized performance tracking
- Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience
- Communicating important updates and support
- Compliance with legal obligations

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- Sensitive information is stored securely on our servers
- We implement industry-standard security measures
- Regular security audits are conducted
- You can request data deletion at any time

## 5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:
- Cloud storage (AWS, Google Cloud)
- Analytics (Firebase, Mixpanel)
- Crash reporting (Sentry)
- Payment processing (Stripe)

These services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information:
- With service providers who assist our operations
- When required by law or to protect our rights
- With your explicit consent

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Withdraw consent at any time

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

## 9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide services. You can request deletion, and we will comply within 30 days (except where legally required to retain).
